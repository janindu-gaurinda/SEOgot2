Top Tourist Destinations in Sri Lanka

From misty mountains to golden beaches, explore the best places to visit in Sri Lanka with our hand-picked travel experiences and detailed destination guides.

Anuradhapura

Known as the "Ancient City of Kings," Anuradhapura is one of Sri Lanka's most sacred cities, featuring massive stupas, ancient ruins, and the oldest known living tree—Sri Maha Bodhi.

Bentota

Popular for river safaris, luxury resorts, and golden beaches, Bentota is a favorite coastal destination in Sri Lanka for relaxation and adventure seekers.

Trincomalee

A stunning East Coast destination with crystal-clear beaches, diving at Pigeon Island Marine Park, and the cliff-top Koneswaram Temple offering breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.
